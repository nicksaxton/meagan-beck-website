My name is Meagan Beck and I am a journalist.

When I was younger, I would make fake newspapers for my parents to read with made up stories. Little did I know, what I loved doing as a hobby when I was a kid would soon become my career.

My portfolio is a look at some of the best work I have produced as an editorial intern for The Grand Rapids Press (MLive), reporter for MSU's student newspaper The State News and as an editorial intern for Indianapolis Monthly magazine.

My name is Meagan and I am a 23 year old journalist from the Lansing area. In May 2016, I graduated from Michigan State University with bachelor of arts degree in journalism and a specialization in editorial reporting.

I have experience with general news and breaking news reporting but my favorite style of writing is features. I enjoy writing profiles on people and places.

Aside from writing, my hobbies include going to concerts, watching MSU basketball games, trying out new baking recipes and traveling.
